1. How do I find a game?

You can browse our collection by scrolling through the homepage or exploring different categories. Each game includes a short description to help you choose.

2. Can I buy games directly from this website?

No. PixelPlay Arcade is a school project and does not sell or distribute games. The content is for educational purposes only.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No account is required. All features of this website are freely accessible.

4. Are the games playable on this site?

No. The games shown here are for presentation only. This site does not provide downloads or online play.

5. Who owns the content shown on this website?

All games, images, and trademarks belong to their respective owners. PixelPlay Arcade does not claim ownership of any third-party content.

6. Can I request a specific game to be featured?

Since this is a school project, requests cannot be added. The games shown are for demonstration purposes only.

7. Is this website safe to use?

Yes. This site does not collect personal data, process payments, or distribute software. It is strictly for educational use.

8. Why can’t I download the games here?

Because this is not a real online store. The games are displayed as examples only and cannot be downloaded or purchased here.

9. How often is the site updated?

The site is updated occasionally as part of the school project requirements, not on a regular schedule.

10. Who can I contact for more information?

If you have questions, please use the contacts at the footer of the website to reach the project creator.
